About us

About Twables

Travel Made Smarter for Canadian Business

Twables was founded with a single goal: to take the hassle out of corporate travel for Canadian companies. We believe business travel should be easy to manage, cost-efficient, and completely transparent—without relying on outdated systems, messy spreadsheets, or inflexible booking portals.

Our platform is built for operations leaders, finance teams, and growing companies who want full visibility into how their team travels—without giving up flexibility or control.

Who We Are

We’re a Canadian-owned travel technology company with a passion for simplicity and clarity. Our team includes professionals from corporate travel, tech, finance, and customer experience—all united by the belief that managing business trips should be as seamless as booking a personal vacation. We know the unique needs of Canadian companies, and our platform reflects that: Canadian billing, bilingual support, CRA-compliant invoicing, and integrations that work with your existing tools

What We Do

Twables is a web-based travel management platform that helps Canadian companies:

  • Book flights, hotels, and ground transport from one centralized dashboard
  • Apply custom travel policies and approval flows automatically
  • Track real-time spending across teams, departments, and projects
  • Generate consolidated invoices and tax-ready receipts in CAD
  • Get 24/7 support from a real, human team—no bots, no hold music

Whether you’re sending a single team member to a client pitch or organizing a nationwide leadership retreat, Twables gives you the tools to do it confidently.

Why Canadian Companies Choose Us

  • Made for Canada –CAD billing, local compliance, and bilingual support
  • No App Required –Everything runs smoothly in your browser—on desktop or mobile
  • Total Visibility – Know where your team is, how much you're spending, and where to optimize
  • Built-in Flexibility –Modify or cancel bookings directly, with access to flexible fares
  • Real Support, Real People – You get personalized help before, during, and after every trip

We’re here to help businesses of all sizes—from startups booking their first cross-province meetings to enterprises coordinating multi-city events.

Our Values

  • Clarity: Transparent pricing, policies, and reporting
  • Trust: Reliable support and a secure platform
  • Efficiency: Fewer steps, smarter tools, better outcomes
  • Local Insight: A deep understanding of the Canadian business travel landscape
  • Human-Centered: We build tools people want to use, not just what IT teams tolerate

Our Vision

To be Canada's most trusted platform for corporate travel—one that makes every trip smoother, every policy clearer, and every dollar easier to account for.

We're not here to replace your team—we're here to empower it
