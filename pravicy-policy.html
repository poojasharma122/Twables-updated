Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

Twables Technologies Inc. (“Twables”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information in connection with our corporate travel management platform, accessible via www.twables.com (the “Platform”).

By using our Platform or engaging with our services, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to all users of the Twables Platform, including corporate clients, travelers, administrators, and guests accessing the service from Canada or abroad. It complies with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and other relevant regulations.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

We collect personal and company information when you:

  • Create an account or profile

  • Book or manage travel services

  • Fill out a form or submit inquiries

  • Contact our support team

  • Participate in events or surveys

Examples include:

  • Name, email address, phone number

  • Company name, role, and billing information

  • Traveler profiles (preferences, frequent flyer numbers)

  • Payment details (via secure payment processors)

  • Itinerary and booking information

b. Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Platform, we may collect certain technical data automatically, such as:

  • Browser type and device information

  • IP address and geographic location

  • Usage patterns and activity logs

  • Cookies and session tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage our travel services

  • Process bookings and payments

  • Customize user experiences and preferences

  • Maintain security and troubleshoot performance

  • Send transactional communications and updates

  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

  • Improve our platform, tools, and offerings

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party travel providers (e.g., airlines, hotels, ground transport) to fulfill bookings

  • Payment processors for secure transactions

  • Authorized team members within your organization

  • Service providers and partners who support our platform (e.g., IT, analytics, CRM systems)

  • Legal authorities, if required by law, court order, or regulation

All third parties are required to comply with strict data protection and confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the services you requested

  • Comply with legal and financial record-keeping requirements

  • Resolve disputes or enforce our agreements

You may request deletion of your data in accordance with Section 9 below.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience, analyze usage, and remember preferences. You may modify cookie settings through your browser, but some features of the Platform may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from:

  • Unauthorized access

  • Loss or misuse

  • Alteration or destruction

These measures include data encryption, role-based access, secure hosting, and regular security audits

8. International Transfers

Although Twables is a Canadian company, some of our third-party partners may be located in other jurisdictions. Where applicable, we ensure that international data transfers comply with privacy laws and contractual protections.

9. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you

  • Correct or update inaccurate data

  • Request deletion of your personal information

  • Withdraw consent to data processing (where applicable)

  • File a complaint with a privacy authority

To make a request, email us at support@twables.com. We may require identity verification before proceeding.

10. Marketing Communications

You may receive occasional updates, promotions, or newsletters from Twables. You can opt out at any time by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails

  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

  • Contacting us directly

We do not send marketing emails to employees unless explicitly subscribed.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adult business use only and are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Platform after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
