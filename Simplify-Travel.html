Simplify Travel

Smart Business Travel for Small Teams

Managing travel for a small business doesn’t have to be messy. Twables helps Canadian companies take control of flights, hotels, and expenses—without adding extra workload or complexity.

Whether you’re a startup booking your first sales trips or a growing team coordinating multiple travelers, Twables gives you the tools to book efficiently, stay on budget, and scale with ease.

Challenges Small Businesses Face with Travel

Small businesses often deal with:

  • Disorganized bookings across multiple tools
  • Employees booking travel ad hoc and outside of policy
  • Confusion over approval processes and expense tracking
  • Lack of visibility into where and how money is being spent
  • No centralized system for reconciliation or receipts

These issues lead to overspending, delays in reimbursement, and extra work for admin and finance teams.

How Twables Solves It

Twables was built to help small businesses operate like large ones—without the overhead.

  • Everything in One PlaceBook flights, hotels, and transport on a single web-based platform—available in your browser, no app needed.
  • Simple, Flexible Travel PoliciesCreate and enforce custom travel rules for your team. Set spending limits, define approval flows, and ensure compliance automatically.
  • Real-Time VisibilityTrack travel activity and expenses as they happen. Get a clear view of upcoming trips and total spend.
  • Automated Invoicing and ReceiptsStop chasing paperwork. Twables delivers CRA-ready receipts and consolidated CAD invoices.
  • No Setup Costs or Complex OnboardingSign up, add your team, and start booking within minutes. We’ll guide you through setup if you need help.

Benefits for Canadian Small Businesses

  • Save time on manual admin
  • Maintain oversight without micromanaging
  • Improve employee experience with smooth booking and quick reimbursements
  • Gain leverage with access to competitive rates for flights and hotels
  • Stay tax-compliant with Canadian financial standards

Travel Management That Grows With You

Twables is ideal for:

  • Founders and small teams making frequent domestic trips

  • Businesses scaling from a few trips per year to multiple trips per month
