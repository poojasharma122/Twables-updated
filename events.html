Corporate Team-Building Travel & Events

Plan Rewarding Trips That Build Teams and Business

Twables helps Canadian companies plan and manage corporate travel for team-building events, off-site meetings, and group retreats—including destinations with luxury hotels, entertainment, and land-based casinos. Whether you’re motivating your sales team or hosting an executive retreat, we make it easy to organize travel and stay on budget.

01 Why Team Travel Matters

Team-building travel is more than a perk—it’s a strategic investment in culture, trust, and performance. Done right, it energizes teams, deepens relationships, and celebrates achievements in a way that in-office activities simply can’t.

But coordinating flights, hotels, group bookings, and activities can become time-consuming and chaotic without the right platform. That’s where Twables comes in.
